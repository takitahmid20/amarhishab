Button Anatomy

These are the small visual details to keep whenever you design primary actions in this project.

1. Top highlight layer

A soft brighter strip sits on the upper half of the button, so the button does not look flat.

2. Soft inner bottom edge

The lower part is slightly deeper to create a pressed but elegant mobile-app feel.

3. Large radius + quiet shadow

The button should feel friendly and modern, not sharp or too web-generic.
