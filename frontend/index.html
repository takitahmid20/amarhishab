Why we built this

We believe every taka deserves a record.

AmarHishab started with a simple problem — students and young adults in Bangladesh had no easy way to track daily micro-expenses. Bus fare, tea, snacks, rickshaw — these small costs add up fast, but traditional tools are too slow or complicated. So we built AmarHishab: a quick-entry finance tracker that works the way you actually spend.
